Hello This Is The About Me Page
    I used to be a huge fan of Kanye West's production but lately it seems that he doesn't have much to say outside of hysterical impulsive psychological flailing. I used to think that it takes a creature of extreme intellect to produce the oratory craftsmanship that he was able to bring to life, listening to his debut album was akin to  moses witnessing the burning bush. It hurts me to see talent and ideas so unique be devoured by  an insatiable industry.
